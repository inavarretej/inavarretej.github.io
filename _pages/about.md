I am pursuing my PhD within the
[Laboratoire de Géologie](https://www.geologie.ens.fr/) at the
[École normale supérieure - PSL](https://www.ens.psl.eu/),
under the supervision of
[Prof. Jean-Arthur Olive](https://jaolive.weebly.com) and [Prof. Eric Calais](https://www.geologie.ens.fr/~ecalais/).

After earning a degree in Mathematical Civil Engineering from the
Universidad de Concepción, Chile, I joined the doctoral network
[TALENTS](https://www.talents-dn.eu) to conduct research on the mechanical and thermal
evolution of the lithosphere during early continental rifting.

My work combines geophysical observations, numerical modeling,
analytical solutions, and data-driven techniques to investigate
the interplay between tectonics, magmatism, and lithospheric
rheology. This research aims to improve our understanding of the
physical processes controlling the initiation and evolution of
continental rifts and to advance quantitative methods for
lithospheric deformation analysis.
